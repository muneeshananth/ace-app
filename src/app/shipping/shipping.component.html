Returns

Everything about our hassle-free returns & refunds.

How long do I have to return an item?

You have 15 days from the date of purchase to return any item that meets our return policy.

What is the return policy?

  • We'll refund an item(s) that is accompanied by the original proof of purchase such as your receipt or tax invoice.
  • The returned item(s) must be in good and unused condition, in its original sealed packaging and with all paperwork, parts and accessories that came along with it.
  • We'll offer your full refund via cash or any other convenient payment method.

Are there any items excluded from the return policy?

The following products and services are non-refundable and non-exchangeable:

  • Special order or customized items (example: Paint, products sold per metre, cut timber).
  • Damaged or used items, which are no longer in a sellable condition.
  • Items by reason of their nature (DVDs, CDs, bedding); perishable goods (food, drinks, plants, magazines) and personal hygiene products (shavers, toothbrush and headphones).
  • Installed and assembled items, except for faulty items.
  • Delivery charges, except for faulty items.

How do I return my items?

The only way to return your item is to arrange for a pick-up by calling us at ACE (222 12 229).
We will soon have better means for you to return items. Stay tuned!

What should I do if my order arrives damaged, defective or if the wrong item was delivered?

If you notice any visible damage to the item on delivery, refuse the delivery and call us at ASK ACE (222 12 229). If you find the item is damaged after you've opened it, immediately return it to your nearest ACE store or call us to arrange a pick-up of your damaged item(s).

Can I return an item that was purchased under promotion or came with a bundle deal?

You can return promotional or bundled items, but you'll also have to return any free or discounted product that came along with the original item. We don't offer a partial refund on a promotional bundle.

Are there any items excluded from the return policy?

Your refund will be processed via your original payment method.

If I've paid through my debit or credit card, how long would it take for me to get my refund?

The whole process right from receiving your returned item(s), to running a check on the item(s) and your bank processing the return will roughly 2 to 7 days.

DO I NEED TO REGISTER OR CREATE AN ACCOUNT TO PURCHASE FROM ACEKUWAIT.COM?

No, you can purchase from our web site without creating an account. This is called shopping as a "Guest". However, setting up an account with us does offer some advantages with a register account, you can:

  1. Review your pending orders and your purchase history
  2. Track your pending orders
  3. Save time and streamline your checkout process
  4. Manage your address book and email settings
  5. Potentially qualify for special promotions
  6. Avail of future services that will be created for Registered Users only

Additional Information:

Exclusive Delivery Service*

With our world-class service, we assure delivery within 24hrs. Exclusive Delivery Service* is exclusively available only on acekuwait.com for Kuwait based online customers. If the delivery exceeds the 24hrs timeframe, delivery charges will be on us.

Here's how Exclusive Delivery Service* works:

  • Shop on acekuwait.com.
  • Once you have selected an item, click the 'Add to Cart button' and you will see if this item is able to be shipped to your home. You can choose ship to home in your cart.
  • Continue to proceed through the checkout process through to order completion.
  • Once the order is placed, you will receive an Order Confirmation email with your shipment tracking information.
  • After you complete your Ship-to-Home order, the receipt page provides you with your order number and information about the order. Keep your order number in a safe place; it will be needed for all order references.

There are shipping charges associated with Ship-to-Home orders. Shipping costs for orders placed on acehardware.com are based on the weight of the total order. An order containing multiple items typically receives a volume-shipping discount and therefore your total shipping and handling cost may be lower than the sum of individual ship charges appearing in your cart. You will be provided with the total shipping cost for your order during checkout.

*Any order exceeding 24hrs timeframe will not be charged for delivery. This excludes any delays due to customer requests and national holidays and will be calculated as per Kuwait business days.

Please note, business days are Sunday & Thursday.
